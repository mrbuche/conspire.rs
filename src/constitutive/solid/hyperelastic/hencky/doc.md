The Hencky hyperelastic constitutive model.

**Parameters**
- The bulk modulus $`\kappa`$.
- The shear modulus $`\mu`$.

**External variables**
- The deformation gradient $`\mathbf{F}`$.

**Internal variables**
- None.

**Notes**
- The Hencky strain measure is given by $`\mathbf{h}=\tfrac{1}{2}\ln(\mathbf{B})`$.
